A, a <n, m>

the first letter (Capital A, and small letter a)
	    of the Khasi Alphabet. It is a sign of a Vowel
	    (Sawa-hi). Its sound is very open, more than the a
	    of the English exclamation Ah!
a! <int>
A!, Ah!, Oh!
indicating joy, sorrow, surprise, warning
         Note: In English the interjections A, Ah are written
	    with a capital letter, but it does not seem justified
	    to follow this rule also in Khasi.
'a <n, f>
kapok tree, silk tree, simul tree, vegetable silk tree, Bombax malabarica
   See also: {kya}

         Note: The Apostrophe (') is often used in Khasi to indicate
	  that some letter or letters have been omitted, specially in compound words:
	  ar'ti-ar'jat, 'tiewkulab, for arkti-arkjat, syntiew-kulab.
